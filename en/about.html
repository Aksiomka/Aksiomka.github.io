About an artist

I am a hyperrealist artist, born and living in Saint-Petersburg. I paint in oil. I've been painting extensively for the last 7 years. I studied with professional artists. Since 2024, I have been actively participating in group exhibitions.

Since December 2024, I’ve been a member of the Artists Trade Union of Russia.

 

The main characters of my paintings are flowers, as they are one of the most beautiful creations of nature. I convey my ideas through flowers, urge you to think about this or that topic. I deliberately make my works aesthetically attractive in order to reach out to the maximum number of viewers.

I paint in such a way that you want to smell the flowers and eat the sweets right away. Oil painting allows me to achieve such realism that allows the viewer to look at familiar things a little differently.

My art encourages you to reflect on current topics that many people face. These are loneliness and acceptance of a person in society, temptation and self-control, excessive and conscious consumption. In my paintings, I convey my view and my experiences, as these topics are very relevant for me.
